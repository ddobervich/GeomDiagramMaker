Shapes
Press 'h' for help

Controls

  • Add shape — Drag a shape from the palette onto the workspace.
  • Delete shape — Drag a shape to the trash icon in the palette.
  • Move vertex — Drag a vertex to change the shape.
  • Rotate shape — Hold Ctrl and drag a vertex (cursor shows rotation icon).
  • Scale shape — Hold Shift and drag a vertex.
  • Vertex menu — Single-click a vertex for point size, color, “Draw an altitude”, and “Right angle mark” (if the angle is near 90°).
  • Edge menu — Single-click an edge for line width, style (solid/dashed/dotted), and color.
  • Draw altitude — From the vertex menu choose “Draw an altitude”, then click an edge. Double-click an altitude line to edit its label.
  • Vertex & edge labels — Drag labels to reposition them.
  • Undo / Redo — Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y.

Press h or Escape to close
